    A World Changed by a Virus

    The unprecedented effects of the Covid 19 Pandemic were spread far and wide across the globe. Significant changes were seen in how we managed finances, health, or habits, behaviour and more. This website aims to take you through some of the facts and figures that came about from this pandemic. This journalistic story will be visualised across three pages of data, where the spotlight will be pointed at economic, healthcare, and household examples of how the world looked pre and post pandemic.
