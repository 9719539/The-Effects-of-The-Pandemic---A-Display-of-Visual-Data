    A Diagnosis of Healthcare

    Our hospitals and medical clinics faced an unprecedented wave of changes as they moved to adapt to a new challenge not seen in decades. Changes were made to how our hopsitals were run, what was prioritised, how resources were managed and even the administration of new and returning staff. As hospitals adapted and changed their approach, the data they left behind truly demonstrated just how impactful the pandemic was on our healthcare system.
